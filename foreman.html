Biography

Tyson Fury is a boxer from England. He is famous for unifying the the heavyweight titles. His record is almost perfect with only one draw in thirty-three fights. Although he dealt with adversity throughout his career he has always fought through it and succeeded.

Even though he now stands 6'9", Tyson started his life off premature and only weighed one pound. Tyson's father named him after another famous boxer Mike Tyson. His dad decided on the name because before that he and his wife had already lost two other children to premature birth. Tyson Fury was raised fighting within the Traveller community before beginning as an amateur. He has a lineage of both bare-knuckle and professional boxers in his family.
